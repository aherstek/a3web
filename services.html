Services

Geological Services

Our GIS consulting company provides comprehensive geological services, specializing in geological engineering and geomorphological mapping. We leverage advanced technologies and skilled geologists to conduct detailed surveys for projects such as dam construction in Surata. Our expertise extends to assessing natural hazards in areas like Bolivar and Cienaga Zapatosa, contributing valuable insights to environmental management. Whether it's understanding the Earth's composition, studying geological features, or supporting construction projects, our geological services ensure informed decision-making.

Geophysical Solutions

Our geophysical services utilize cutting-edge technology to study the Earth's subsurface and physical properties. Through methods like seismic surveys, gravity measurements, and electromagnetic studies, our geophysicists provide crucial data for industries ranging from energy exploration to environmental monitoring. Our geophysical solutions contribute to resource exploration, seismic hazard assessment, and informed decision-making in diverse sectors. We employ advanced geophysical techniques to uncover valuable information about the Earth's interior, supporting a wide range of applications

Telemetry and Wildlife Tracking

As part of our geospatial offerings, we specialize in telemetry services, particularly in wildlife tracking. Using state-of-the-art technology, including GPS tracking, Iridium satellite tracking, and digital circuitry, we monitor and collect data remotely from inaccessible locations. Our telemetry services play a vital role in wildlife research, allowing for the study of animal movements, migration patterns, and ecological interactions. Whether it's for ecological research, conservation efforts, or environmental monitoring, our telemetry services provide real-time data transmission and analysis for informed decision-making.

Earth and Space

Earth and Space services includes advanced weather monitoring and prediction, utilizing cutting-edge technology for satellite imagery analysis, such as classifying landscapes post-natural disasters. Our transit suitability analysis extends beyond Earth, examining regions for potential GO Transit expansion. Moreover, we delve into space mission and design, exemplified by our Mission Level Phase 0 Proposal for the Io Geothermal Orbital Observation Satellite. This initiative aims to provide in-depth observations of Jupiter's moon, Io, and analyze its volcanic activity, leveraging knowledge and software adapted from Earth's well-studied volcanoes. Our commitment extends to linking informative PDFs for enriched content on our site
